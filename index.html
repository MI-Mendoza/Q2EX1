Momentum

Momentum can be defined as "mass in motion." All objects have mass; so if an object is moving, then it has momentum - it has its mass in motion.

Impulse

Impulse is a certain amount of force you apply for a certain amount of time to cause a change in momentum.

Valence Electrons

Valence electrons are the electrons located at the outermost shell of an atom
